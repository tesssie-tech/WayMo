Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI recommendation work?

Our AI analyzes your past orders, ratings, and browsing behavior to suggest dishes that match your unique taste profile. The more you use WayMo, the smarter it gets!

Is there a delivery fee?

Delivery fees vary based on distance and restaurant policies. However, WayMo Pro members enjoy free delivery on all orders over $15.

Can I track my order in real-time?

Absolutely! Once your order is confirmed, you can watch your delivery driver's progress on our live map from the kitchen to your doorstep.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cash on Delivery for select locations.
